Project Description Analysis

Purpose: To gather as much information as possible from the project description and begin planning effective research strategies. This is also an opportunity to identify information that you do not yet know or need to know to make your project successful. Your project description provides crucial information about your project. As a team, analyze it and answer the following questions. Prepare the information in whatever format you think most useful for your work (lists, tables, paragraphs, etc.). About the Project: Goals: What does the sponsor hope to gain from the project? Insights into the needs/desires of local tourists and how to encourage/attract more local tourism to the area Brand the village in a positive light and determine its most desirable assets Underlying problem: What motivates the project? Why is it important (to sponsor and others)? What is the larger societal issue the project is addressing?

Struggling to attract local tourists to visit village and use their facilities We are addressing the livelihood of inn owners and those who work in the area The pandemic has caused these local places to lose valuable tourists. This trend can also be seen around the world, so this project addresses a larger global issue as well Forming a cohesive identity as a group of auberges on the regional and eventually international tourism market Major booking companies have a monopoly on the tourism market preventing locals from profiting from tourists.

Objectives:

What specific objectives, tasks, or questions does the sponsor envision for the project (i.e., big steps necessary to complete the project)? Identify needs/desires of local tourists through survey/feedback Understand how local business has been affected by the pandemic, and how to combat this Create new branding and a logo for the business to gain more attraction, and have a more interactive surface when tourists are not on site Document the lives of the small business owners through thoughtful communication and storytelling Develop a platform to support the auberges’ businesses through streamlining information, advertisement, and booking services while keeping the humanity of the owners/employees a central part of the marketing

Stakeholders:

Who will your project impact? Identify the internal and external stakeholders of your project? Project will impact locals, small business owners (auberge owners), their employees, and those who will travel to the village Internally - people in the towns, auberge owners Externally - the town itself (reputation, marketing, “logo”), local tourists and foreign tourists, Where does the sponsor fit in ?

Methods:

What techniques does the sponsor suggest you use to meet the project objectives (field work, surveys, interviews, database development, etc.)? Interviews with the inn owners/employees Interviews with local and foreign tourists Database development/research to help create the platform that inn owners and tourists can use Field work to get an idea of the area and determine desirable assets to help the brand

Outcomes:

What tangible products does the sponsor envision you will create by the end of the project? A platform that the auberges can use to share their accommodation information, and also serve as a way for tourists to book their products Brand and logo

What new information do you need to learn/know?

New Research Questions/Topics: Based on the above information, make a list of questions or topics you believe will help you organize your project research. Try brainstorming lots of questions first, then organize them thematically.
What currently makes the village and these inn desirable for local/foreign tourists? How can we make the process of booking trips efficient for tourists? What is the most effective way to market the village? What are other platforms currently being used to book trips? What are their commissions of them? How is it affecting inn owners? How do auberges operate? Before and during COVID? What can the rest of the village gain from improving the auberges tourism market? Why do previous tourists choose to stay at competing booking platform? How has COVID impacted the attendance of tourists to the area and to the auberges? What did tourism look like before the pandemic hit? Is there a main reason other than travel that tourists do not come to the area? What are the auberge's strongest features? Can these be improved? What is tourism like in Morocco How much money does morocco generate from tourism What are attitudes about traveling (esp with covid) for moroccans
